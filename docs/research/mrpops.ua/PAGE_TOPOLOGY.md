# Mr. Pops Page Topology

1. Fixed header with logo, cart indicator, taste map link, and menu trigger.
2. Hero with long introductory quote, marquee-like CTA strip, and dual hero imagery.
3. Brand story block with two stacked headings, descriptive copy, and about link.
4. Popular flavors product grid with add-to-cart buttons.
5. Gift box purchase CTA with supporting copy and video.
6. Partnership section with large editorial image and three explanatory text blocks.
7. Sales points / map invitation block with CTA.
8. Footer contact form, legal links, social links, and credit.

Interaction model:

- Global shell: fixed header plus smooth scrolling.
- Hero and section reveals: time-driven / scroll-assisted animations from the mirrored runtime.
- Product area: click-driven buttons and links.
- Footer form: input-driven form controls.
